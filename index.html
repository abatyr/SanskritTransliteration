Welcome to online Devanagari converter
This converter is based on the conventional Harvard-Kyoto transliteration system which has been extended for Cyrillic so you may input both Latin and Cyrillic symbols. Please check the transliteration table for details.
To separate two conjunct vowels use the dot: e.g. saJjaya.uvAca.
It is highly recommended to have Sanskrit 2003 font installed on your computer to display Devanagari correctly.
